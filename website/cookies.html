Cookies Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Summary: FixOnce does not use cookies or any tracking technology.

No Cookies

FixOnce runs entirely on your local machine and does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of browser-based tracking.

No Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any analytics service. There is no usage tracking of any kind.

Local Storage

The FixOnce desktop app may use local browser storage internally to remember UI preferences (such as collapsed panels or theme settings). This data:

  • Stays in your browser
  • Is never transmitted anywhere
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser data

Third-Party Services

FixOnce does not load any third-party scripts, fonts, or resources that could set cookies or track you.
